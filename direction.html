Directions

  1. Start Your Journey at Your Nearest MRT Station

    • The most common MRT line to reach The Linq @ Beauty World is the Downtown Line (Blue Line).

  2. Get Off at Beauty World MRT Station (DT5)

    • The Beauty World MRT Station is directly connected to The Linq @ Beauty World, and it is located on the Downtown Line (DT5).

  3. Walk to The Linq

    • The Linq is located within the Beauty World Centre, a shopping mall right next to the MRT station. From Exit B, you will see clear signage guiding you to The Linq @ Beauty World.
