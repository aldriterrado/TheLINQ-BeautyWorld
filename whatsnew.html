The LINQ @ Beauty World is an exciting new development located in the vibrant Beauty World precinct, offering a mix of residential, retail, and lifestyle spaces. The soft opening period is a time for businesses within The LINQ to fine-tune their operations, while allowing early visitors to experience the spaces and services before the grand opening.
